# Cloud-Journey
Welcome to my cloud computing journey!

"Cloud-Journey" is the place that I will share my transition out of healthcare and into cloud computing! I'm feeling excited and ready to begin! I am turning 40 later this year and will be moving away from a 20 year career in a forward-facing, patient-focused setting. By my estimate, I have another 20+ years left in the workplace and I can't think of a better way to spend it than learning something new that excites me!
